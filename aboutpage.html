This can sometimes be pretty cool when I'm learning something new within my hobbies. When I'm sewing, I get to learn about historical constuming and some bit of mechanical engineering. (Sewing machines are magic.) When I'm learning about rollerskating, I get all of the information about how not to break my arms, legs, and/or skull before I strap my skates on.
Programming, though, is different. I have to accept so much magic at the beginning in order to get my beginner project done. Still, each time I learn something new, I have the joy of going back and making something that worked into something that is efficent and maybe even a little elegant.
I'm excited to learn about web development so I can use these creative design skills I've developed in my other hobbies. Also, it's all a big puzzle. The person who wants to know all things can't simply leave a puzzle unfinished.
